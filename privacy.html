Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 24, 2026

Baby Sleep Recorder is designed as a local-only baby sleep tracker. We do not collect, sell, share, or transmit personal data.

Data Storage

All sleep records, baby profile information, notes, reminders, and local album photos are stored only on your device. The app does not require an account and does not use cloud sync.

Data Collection

Baby Sleep Recorder does not collect analytics, advertising identifiers, contact information, location data, health data, or any other personal data from this app.

Local Photos

Photos added to the app's local album stay on your device. They are not uploaded to a server by Baby Sleep Recorder.

Deleting Data

If you delete the app or reset baby data inside the app, local app data may be removed from this device. This does not affect your iPhone Photos library or other apps.

Children's Privacy

The app is intended for parents and caregivers. It does not knowingly collect personal data from children or adults.

Contact

If you have questions or feedback, please use the Baby Sleep Recorder App Store page after launch.
